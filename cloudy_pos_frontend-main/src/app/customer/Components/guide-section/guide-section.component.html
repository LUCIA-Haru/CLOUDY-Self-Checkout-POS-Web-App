Mobile Checkout in Five Simple Steps

Choose Scan ‘N’ Thru for effortless self-checkout. Our solution streamlines transactions and improves customer satisfaction with easy scanning and payment.

📱

Step 1: Download the App

Customers install the store’s app on their smartphone to get started.

🔍

Step 2: Scan Products

Customers scan product barcodes or QR codes with their smartphone as they shop.

🛒

Step 3: Add to Cart

Items are instantly added to the virtual cart for real-time tracking.

💳

Step 4: Pay with a Tap

Customers review their cart and complete payment directly in the app.

🚪

Step 5: Exit with Receipt

A digital receipt and exit pass are generated for a hassle-free exit.
